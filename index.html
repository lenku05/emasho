A Secluded Haven Awaits

Welcome to Emasho Camp Resort, one designed to anticipate your every mood, delight senses and foster deep connection to natural surrounding. More than just a resort, Emasho celebrates deeply rooted Kenyan Maasai Culture, best Nyamachoma, Kuku and a lot more. Leave your worries behind and create unforgetable memories.

Culinary
Emasho Hotel invites you for a culinary experience in an enchanting setting, at any time of the day, from lunch to dinner. With a responsible approach to the culinary arts, Chef Roberto serves fine gourmet meals that reflect harmony and happiness beyond the kitchen.
